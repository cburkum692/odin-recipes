Starter

I first started making my own starter when everyone else did, in March 2020 when the world was ending. I came across the method in the tinystarter protocol and followed that since it didn't require a lot of flour. I've sinced moved up to larger volumes of starter but still follow roughly the same idea, with a 1:1:1 mix of starter: flour: water

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup AP flour
  • 1/4 cup filtered water

Steps

  1. mix water and flour in small jar
  2. let sit with lid loosely on for 24 hrs
  3. next day, remove half of mix, and add back same volume of 1:1 flour to water
  4. after about 4 days should see bubbles from yeast
