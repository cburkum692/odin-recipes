Scones

These delicious scones are like eating sweetened pillows with morsels of chocolate in them. They are lighter in texture than scones I've eaten before, almost doughnut-like. But, so, so good. The fact that they use some of the starter discard makes them that much better.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups AP flour
  • 3 tblsp granulated sugar
  • 1 tblsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp kosher salt
  • 5 tblsp cold unsalted butter, cubed
  • 1/2 cup mix ins like dried cranberries or apricots and chocolate chips
  • 1 cup starter discard
  • 1/2 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 1 tblsp sugar

Steps

  1. preheat oven to 425 & line sheet with parchment paper
  2. in food processor, mix flour, sugar, baking powder and salt. Add cold butter & mix. put mix in large bowl.
  3. in another bowl, mix sourdough discard and cream until combined.
  4. Add discard mix to flour mix
  5. knead a few times until dough is formed.
